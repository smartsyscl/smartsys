# **App Name**: SmartSYS Digital Solutions

## Core Features:

- Service Catalog: Showcase of offered digital services like automation and business solutions.
- Testimonial Section: Displaying customer testimonials and success stories.
- Contact Form: Easy contact form submission with pre-filled options.
- Portfolio Showcase: Display a portfolio of successful automation projects.
- SEO Optimization: Implement a basic SEO strategy for higher search engine ranking.
- Service Recommendation Tool: AI-powered tool for suggesting relevant services based on user-provided business needs. Spanish as a principal language and english as a secondary

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep blue (#3F51B5) to represent trust and professionalism.
- Background color: Very light blue (#E8EAF6) for a clean and modern feel.
- Accent color: Soft purple (#9575CD) to add a touch of sophistication.
- Headline font: 'Poppins', a geometric sans-serif font to evoke a precise, contemporary and fashionable style.
- Body font: 'Inter', a grotesque-style sans-serif font with a modern and neutral style.
- Use modern and minimalist icons related to digital services.
- Clean, grid-based layout with clear sections for services, testimonials, and contact information.
- Subtle transition effects for page elements and hover states.
